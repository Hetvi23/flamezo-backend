# Razorpay Setup Guide for Restaurants (Flamezo)

This guide will help you create your Razorpay account and start accepting payments in the easiest way possible.

No technical knowledge required 👍

---

#  STEP 1: Create Your Account

1. Go to: https://dashboard.razorpay.com/
2. Click on **Sign Up**
3. Enter:
   - Mobile Number
   - Email ID
   - Password

---

# 🧾 STEP 2: Choose Business Type (IMPORTANT)

👉 Select **Individual / Proprietorship**

✔ Recommended for ALL restaurants  
✔ Fastest approval  
✔ No complex documents required  

> Even if you have partners, you can still select this option and use one owner’s details.

---

# 👤 STEP 3: Enter Owner Details

Fill details of **ONE person only**:

- Full Name (as per PAN)
- Mobile Number
- Email

---

# 🪪 STEP 4: Upload KYC Documents

## 1. PAN Card
- Upload clear image of PAN card

## 2. Aadhaar Card
- Linked with mobile number
- OTP verification required

---

# 🏦 STEP 5: Add Bank Account

Enter:

- Account Holder Name (same as PAN)
- Account Number
- IFSC Code

### Upload ANY ONE:
- Cancelled Cheque  
- OR Bank Statement (last 3 months)

---

# 🏢 STEP 6: Business Details

Fill:

- Business Name (your restaurant name)
- Business Type: Restaurant / Food Services
- Business Address

---

# 📄 STEP 7: Business Proof (ANY ONE)

Upload at least one:

- FSSAI License ✅ (best option)
- GST Certificate (if available)
- Shop Act License
- Udyam Certificate

---

# 🌐 STEP 8: Website / App Details

If you don’t have a website:

👉 Use Flamezo link or write:

"We are a restaurant accepting food orders via Flamezo digital menu."

---

# ⚠️ IMPORTANT: Avoid These Mistakes

❌ Do NOT select Partnership or Pvt Ltd unless you have proper documents  
❌ Do NOT use different person’s bank account  
❌ Do NOT upload blurry images  
❌ Do NOT mismatch PAN name and bank name  

---

# 🔐 STEP 9: Submit & Wait for Approval

- Approval time: 1–3 working days  
- You will get confirmation on email  

---

# 🔑 STEP 10: Get API Keys (For Flamezo)

1. Go to Dashboard → Settings → API Keys  
2. Generate:
   - Key ID  
   - Key Secret  

📌 Share these keys with Flamezo team

---

# ❓ Special Cases

## If You Have Multiple Owners
👉 Still use **ONE owner’s details (recommended)**  
OR  
👉 Upload authorization letter if required

---

## If You See "Power of Attorney" Screen

👉 This means:
- Someone else is doing KYC  
- OR wrong business type selected  

✔ Solution:
- Use owner details  
- OR switch to Proprietorship  

---

👉 Faster approval
