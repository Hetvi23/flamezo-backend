# Flamezo: The Unified Restaurant OS & Growth Engine (2026)

Flamezo is not just a digital menu; it is a **Unified Restaurant Operating System**. We bridge the gap between in-store dining excellence and digital marketing automation, providing restaurant owners with a single dashboard to manage every corner of their business.

---

## 1. The Digital Core: Reimagining the Menu
We transform your static menu into a high-conversion digital experience.

- **Dynamic QR Menus**: Not just a PDF. A real-time, searchable, and beautifully designed interface that stays synced with your kitchen.
- **Video & Reel Menus**: Bring your dishes to life. Upload high-energy short-form videos directly into your menu to trigger hunger and increase order value.
- **AI Dish Generation**: No photoshoot? No problem. Our state-of-the-art AI generates professional-grade food photography from your dish descriptions for just ₹10.
- **Multi-Category Smart Layouts**: Automatically group "Best Sellers," "Must Try," and "Chef's Specials" to guide customer choices.

## 2. Marketing Studio: Turning Customers into Fans
Our "Gold" suite provides tools that were previously only available to global chains.

- **Automated Marketing Flows**: Set up "Set & Forget" campaigns. Automatically send a "We Miss You" WhatsApp message if a customer hasn't visited in 30 days.
- **Customer Segmentation**: Filter your audience by "High Spenders," "First Timers," or "Weekend Regulars" for laser-targeted promotions.
- **Loyalty & Coin System**: A fully integrated loyalty engine. Customers earn "Coins" on every visit, encouraging repeat business without the friction of physical cards.
- **Coupons & Stackable Offers**: Create complex discount logic (e.g., "Free Delivery on orders above ₹500" + "20% Off on Pizzas").

## 3. Growth Intelligence: The Second Brain
Make data-driven decisions with real-time insights.

- **Customer Insights**: Know who your customers are. Track visit frequency, average order value (AOV), and favorite dishes per customer profile.
- **AI Recommendations Engine**: At the point of sale, our AI suggests pairings (e.g., "Customers who bought this Pizza also loved our Garlic Bread") to boost AOV by up to 18%.
- **Google Growth Engine**: Automatically sync your menu to Google Search & Maps. Track profile views, direction clicks, and direct calls from within our dashboard.
- **Review Automation**: Request and monitor Google reviews to maintain a 5-star reputation in your local area.

## 4. Operational Excellence: Efficiency at Scale
Streamline your backend so you can focus on the food.

- **In-Store Table Ordering**: Reduce staff workload. Let customers order and pay directly from their tables using their phones.
- **WhatsApp Direct Ordering**: The easiest way for regulars to order for home delivery—no app download required.
- **Integrated Logistics Hub**: One-click delivery fulfillment via partners like **Borzo** and **Flash**. Track riders in real-time from your dashboard.
- **Payment & POS Harmony**: Integrated payment gateways (Razorpay) with support for UPI, Cards, and Netbanking. Works alongside your existing POS or as a standalone system.
